CC   The improved whole-genome shotgun (WGS) sequences for the genomes
CC   of indica and japonica rice, AAAA02000000 and AACV01000000,
CC   respectively, have multi-megabase contiguity and are nearly
CC   1000-fold improved over the drafts of 2002. Tested against a
CC   non-redundant collection of 19,079 full-length cDNAs, 98.1% of the
CC   genes are aligned without fragmentation to the mapped
CC   super-scaffolds of one or the other genome. Despite having only a
CC   small variation in gene content, there is an enormous variation in
CC   intergenic regions. At least a quarter of the two sequences could
CC   not be aligned, and in the aligned region
CC   single-nucleotide-polymorphism (SNP) rates varied from as little as
CC   3.0 SNP/kb in the coding regions, to 27.6 SNP/kb in the
CC   transposable elements.
CC   Annotation was updated on the chromosomes, CM000126-CM000137, in
CC   December 2008
CC   Contig AAAA02029393 was suppressed in Feb. 2011 because it may be a
CC   contaminant.
